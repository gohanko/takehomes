Welcome to the Naluri Take Home Project!

Here on this page, you will find the URLs to get started with this project!

  • GET /calculating_pi/start - Starts the calculation.
  • GET /calculating_pi/result - The page to get the latest calculation of PI and the circumference of the sun. Keep refreshing to see new ones!
  • GET /calculating_pi/stop - Stops the calculation, but doesn't actually work.
